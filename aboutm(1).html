What is this project about:

We are the students of Computer Engineering. We Are Presenting The Topic Related To Mental Health Awareness... Mental health awareness involves understanding that emotional, psychological, and social well-being are vital to overall health, impacting how people think, feel, and act. It focuses on reducing stigma, recognizing symptoms of conditions like anxiety and depression, and promoting early intervention and access to care, with World Mental Health Day observed annually on October 10 to encourage these efforts. Our aim is to make the Spread awareness about mental health.
